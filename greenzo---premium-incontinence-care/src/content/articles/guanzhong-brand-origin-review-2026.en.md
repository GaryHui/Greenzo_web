## 3. Core Conclusions

- Source: **Local enterprise in Dongguan**, with stable quality inspection and focusing on **economical adult care**.
- Evaluation: **Excellent value for money**, absorption and leakage resistance meet daily/moderate incontinence; **Mediocre texture and durability**, high-end models or frequent changes are required for those with large urine output or long-term bed rest.
- Applicable people: Home, daytime, mild to moderate incontinence elderly/maternity, budget-sensitive users.
