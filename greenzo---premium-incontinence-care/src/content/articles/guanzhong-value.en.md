## Four Practical Strengths

The structure combines a **full core, imported SAP, and fluff pulp** to absorb quickly and reduce rewet risk, helping users stay drier during both daytime activity and overnight rest.

### 2) Breathable, Skin-Friendly Comfort

A soft nonwoven top sheet and breathable backsheet support long-wear comfort and help reduce stuffiness, especially important for elderly, postpartum, and post-op users.

### 3) Leak Protection Design

Double standing leak guards and elastic fit design improve body conformity, reducing side and back leakage in daily movement.

### 4) Broader Size Coverage

Common M / L / XL sizes are available for both men and women, enabling more flexible fit choices across care scenarios.
