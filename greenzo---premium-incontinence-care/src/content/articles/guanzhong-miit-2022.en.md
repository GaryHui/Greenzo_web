# Guanzhong Bedpans And Urinals Listed In The 2022 Elderly Products Promotion Directory

As aging care needs continue to rise, families increasingly prioritize safety, practicality, and long-term value when choosing nursing products. Dongguan Guanzhong Daily Necessities Co., Ltd. was listed in the **2022 Elderly Products Promotion Directory**, with its bedpan and urinal products included in the public list.

## Why This Listing Matters

The directory was formed through enterprise submission, local/industry recommendation, expert review, and public notice. It is widely used as a practical reference for elderly care product quality and applicability.

Guanzhong's bedpan and urinal products were listed under item No. 87, reflecting recognized product capability in elderly daily care scenarios.

## Product Value In Real Care Use

- Safety-oriented materials with practical cleanability and durability.
- Elder-friendly ergonomic structure to reduce discomfort and leakage risk.
- Caregiver-friendly details that support more efficient daily operation.

## Quality Control Foundation

From sourcing and manufacturing to outgoing inspection, Guanzhong applies process-level quality control. The listing further validates the product's safety, practicality, and fit for elderly care use.
